About US

Cuscano is a handicraft business based in Trinidad and Tobago. The items produced are guarantied to be of a high quality and attractive. They are created for your personal use and to meet your gift giving needs. Items produced in the clothing lime include tie dye and hand pained t-shirts for children and adults, Wraps, Shawls and Rompers for the babies. There are various styled bags. In the home decor line there are Table runners, Table napkins and more...
